Siverek Tava

This traditional eastern-Turkey dish is made with ground beef and spices and is suitable for everybody's taste even though it has many spices in it.

Ingredients

  • 250 g medium fat ground beef
  • 1.5 kg of tomatoes
  • 2 red fresh peppers
  • 7-8 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of isot (pepper from Urfa)
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • 1 tablespoon of oil (preferably sunflower)

Steps

  1. All the ingredients are finely chopped. All the ingredients are mixed with the spices.
  2. The mixture is transferred to a tray and flattened.
  3. It is cooked in a preheated oven at 200 degrees until it absorbs its water. Stir with a spoon if needed. Only the top cooking of the oven that is close to cooking is operated and the top is browned. Better in the furnace if available.
